Flow FAQ

From opening a ticket to MO EVV certification

  1. Open a ticket

    Click Open Ticket in the top right. Fill in the issue type, summary, description, and priority, then submit.

  2. Ticket is created in Jira

    On submit, an issue is created in the MO EVV Accruals + Aggregator project with the preset DEV and QA assignees. It appears in the live tables on the next refresh.

  3. The ticket moves through the pipeline

    It starts in DEV statuses; once DEV is complete it moves into QA statuses. The status donuts at the top reflect where everything sits.

  4. Ticket gets certified

    Once QA finishes, the ticket reaches a Done status on the target release line and appears in the MO EVV certified table.
